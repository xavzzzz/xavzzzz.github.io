Statistics

Chocolate is a food that is almost ubiquitous in our daily lives. it is also one of the favorite French especially who are extremely fond of it. For example, in 2014, a Frenchman consumed on average 6 kilos and 950 grams of chocolates. That's half as much as our German neighbors but it remains clearly superior to the Spaniards and Belgians. In addition, 1 in 3 French confesses to eat every day. These trends are valid for both men and women. On the other hand, among seniors, half consumes it every day.
